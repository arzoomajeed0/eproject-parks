About

National Parks Info Portal

Welcome to the National Parks Information Portal, your trusted guide to exploring the natural beauty and biodiversity of Pakistan’s national parks.

Our mission is to provide authentic and easy-to-access information about the country’s most famous parks, highlighting their unique landscapes, animals, and bird species. From the desert and wetlands of Lal Suhanra, to the scenic hills of Kirthar, and the vibrant greenery that surrounds us all.

On this website, you can:

. Learn about the wildlife and birds that make each park special.

. Discover park facilities, maps, and highlights.

. Explore the beauty of nature through detailed guides and visuals.

We believe that raising awareness about wildlife and ecosystems will help promote conservation, eco-tourism, and appreciation of our natural heritage.

Together, let’s discover, protect, and celebrate the treasures of our national parks.

Explore Our Features

Featured Parks

Discover our highlighted national parks, including Lal Suhanra, Ayub, Kirthar, Ayubia, and Khunjerab. Each park offers unique ecosystems, from deserts and wetlands to mountains and forests, showcasing Pakistan’s diverse natural beauty.

Location of Parks

Our portal provides detailed information on the geographical locations of Pakistan’s national parks, including their regions, accessibility, and nearby landmarks, helping you plan your visit with ease.

Animals in Parks

Explore the diverse wildlife inhabiting these national parks, from the Blackbuck and Chinkara in Lal Suhanra to the Snow Leopards and Marco Polo Sheep in Khunjerab, with detailed guides on their habitats and behaviors.

Birds in Parks

Learn about the vibrant bird species across the parks, such as the Houbara Bustard in Lal Suhanra, Himalayan Monal in Ayubia, and Golden Eagles in Khunjerab, with insights into their migratory patterns and habitats.
