Animals of Ayub National Park

Common Leopard

Common Leopards in Ayub’s zoo are apex predators, known for stealth and spotted coats, hunting mammals.

Learn More

Himalayan Black Bear

Himalayan Black Bears in Ayub’s zoo have black fur, white chest patch, feed on berries, insects, mammals.

Learn More

Red Fox

Red Foxes in Ayub’s zoo, with bushy tails, hunt small mammals, birds, insects in varied enclosures.

Learn More

Yellow-Throated Marten

Yellow-Throated Martens in Ayub’s zoo are rare, agile, hunt rodents, birds with colorful fur in zoo.

Learn More

Indian Muntjac

Indian Muntjacs in Ayub’s zoo, small deer, make barking calls, feed on grasses, leaves, fruits daily.

Learn More

Grey Goral

Grey Gorals in Ayub’s zoo, goat-antelopes, navigate rocky slopes, graze on grasses, shrubs in groups.

Learn More

Masked Palm Civet

Masked Palm Civets in Ayub’s zoo are nocturnal, climb with sharp claws, feed on fruits, small animals.

Learn More

Indian Wild Boar

Indian Wild Boars in Ayub’s zoo are hardy, social, root soil, feed on roots, insects, small animals.

Learn More

Indian Porcupine

Indian Porcupines in Ayub’s zoo are nocturnal, have sharp quills, forage for roots, tubers, bark daily.

Learn More
