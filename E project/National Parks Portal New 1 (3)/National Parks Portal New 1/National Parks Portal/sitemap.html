Lal Suhanra National Park

Lal Suhanra, Punjab’s largest park, blends desert, forest, and wetland ecosystems for diverse wildlife.

View on Google Maps

Ayub National Park

Ayub National Park in Rawalpindi offers recreational trails and a zoo, popular for family visits.

View on Google Maps

Kirthar National Park

Kirthar in Sindh mixes mountains and deserts, a scenic reserve for wildlife and rugged trails.

View on Google Maps

Ayubia National Park

Ayubia in Abbottabad, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, features lush forests and trails for nature lovers.

View on Google Maps

Khunjerab National Park

Khunjerab, a high-altitude park in Gilgit-Baltistan, is famed for wildlife and mountain trails.
